Hi I am Ankit Gupta and I love to develop cool applications. I am constantly learning to develop smart applications using artificial intelligence because Artificial Intelligence is the current Big Thing. Another reason for getting into Artificial Intelligence is that this field merges my two favourite subjects i.e Computer Science and Mathematics.

I started my journey as a developer by trying out everything I could. I went for Android Workshops, Hacking workshops, robotics workshop and many more. But after trying all those things I started out with designing Front End for web applications. Soon I realised that I need to learn more because I can't get the data from my users. This drove me to learn Back-End. After that I started noticing that there are things like e-commerce sites which know what we like what we don't like and shows products based on that. I wondered how they did that and started researching, which ultimately led me to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Then I started thinking how Google show the best results so accurately and how it made "Google ASsistant" and wanted to make my own cool personal digital assistant like "Jarvis". This curiosity led me to Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. I also wondered why some businesses fail and why some succeed and constantly try to find the answers to these question. One of the answers I found was the abundance of data but it is very hard to make sense of this data, this led me to find a new friend that is, Data Science.
